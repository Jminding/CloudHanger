<p class="p1">&lt;h2 id="about-cloudhanger"&gt;About CloudHanger&lt;/h2&gt;</p>

<p class="p1">&lt;p&gt;This is a submission for the &lt;a href="https://congressionalappchallenge.us/20-nj09"&gt;2020 Congressional App Challenge&lt;/a&gt;.</p>

<p class="p1">This only contains the UI for the app as the backend is not open source.&lt;/p&gt;</p>

<p class="p1">&lt;h2 id="why-i-decided-to-do-this-project"&gt;Why I decided to do this project&lt;/h2&gt;</p>

<p class="p1">&lt;p&gt;It helps the local community, and especially the local dry cleaning businesses.</p>

<p class="p1">Lately, especially during the Covid-19 pandemic, dry cleaners have suffered.<span class="Apple-converted-space">  </span>This app can hopefully help them streamline their business and bring customers back.&lt;/p&gt;</p>

<p class="p1">&lt;h2 id="what-this-app-does"&gt;What this app does&lt;/h2&gt;</p>

<p class="p1">&lt;p&gt;This app is basically a dry cleaner, but online.<span class="Apple-converted-space">  </span>A dry cleaner uploads their menu and such to the app, where customers can see it.<span class="Apple-converted-space">  </span>Customers can log in to see the status of their clothes, find another dry cleaner, or look at the past transactions.&lt;/p&gt;</p>
